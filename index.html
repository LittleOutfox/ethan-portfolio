01Origin

The fox wakes,
and remembers its name.

I'm Ethan Tiong — Electrical Engineering at the University of Waterloo. I design RTL: state machines, buses, the logic that decides what happens on the next clock edge. FPGA today; silicon is the ambition.

Everything here is true; the fox only carries it. It began with a bear that heard racing hearts and answered with a lullaby. The instinct hasn't changed — only the level of abstraction, which keeps dropping: clean state machines, verification that doesn't flatter, timing that closes honestly. Every tail points the same direction — down, toward the metal.
